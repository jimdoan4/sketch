The Johan Cruijff ArenA is the home of the football club Ajax. On a regular basis, the events calendar includes concerts and dance festivals. In recent years the venue has played host to a wide range of artists during ADE, including Axwell & Ingrosso, Carl Cox, KSHMR, Lost Frequencies, Vini Vici and many more.

Armin van Buuren and Tiësto set to play AMF

Following up on recent announcements of Awakenings hosting no less than ten shows and New Order taking over AFAS Live, we are pleased to continue with news about AMF, where a vast array of electronic music’s biggest names gathers on Saturday October 19th.

The Dutch duo will be accompanied by Alesso and Jonas Blue, with more artist to be announced.

The first announed artists include Armin van Buuren, who topped the DJ Mag Top 100 poll more times than any other artist and Grammy-award winner Tiësto.

Please note this is a high traffic event with limited capacity for ADE Pass holders. Entrance can not be assured/guaranteed.

The one-night event AMF also hosts the crowning of the winner of the DJ Mag Top100, with previous winners including Martin Garrix, Hardwell, David Guetta, and Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike.
